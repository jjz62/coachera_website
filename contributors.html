    The Future of Learning Lab

    The Future of Learning Lab is committed to advancing our understanding of the impact of technology in education and how it can be an engine of equality in society. The Future of Learning Lab takes a multi-disciplinary approach that builds on theories and methods in the learning sciences, cognitive and social psychology, human-computer interaction, economics, and statistical learning. Housed in Computing and Information Science at Cornell University, the lab is directed by Professor Rene Kizilcec.

    Learn more here.

    Mobile Coach

    MobileCoach is an open-source digital intervention program created for the design of scalable digital health interventions. The MobileCoach mobile app served as the code base for CoachEra.
